Ahmad Mohammadi

PhD student at Ruhr University Bochum

I'm a PhD student at Ruhr University Bochum focusing on privacy and trust in large language models (LLMs). My research explores how privacy-preserving techniques and trust mechanisms can ensure the safe and responsible deployment of generative AI systems. I am particularly interested in combining classical privacy concepts, such as differential privacy, with novel approaches to evaluate and enhance user trust in language models.

Research Interests

- Privacy and trust in LLMs
- Trustworthy AI and model transparency
- Differential Privacy
- Privacy‑Preserving Machine Learning
- Federated Learning & Local Differential Privacy
