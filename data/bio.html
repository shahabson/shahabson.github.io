Hi, I'm Ahmad Mohammadi!

PhD Student at Ruhr University Bochum | Privacy & Trustworthiness of LLMs

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm a PhD student at Ruhr University Bochum in the TrustHLT Group, where I research privacy and trustworthiness of large language models (LLMs). My work focuses on membership inference attacks, privacy auditing, and developing methods to evaluate whether specific data was used in LLM training—with applications to copyright protection and privacy assessment.

My Journey

I started with a degree in computer engineering, where I honed my programming and mathematical skills. My path led me to privacy research, where I've worked on differential privacy, privacy-preserving machine learning, and network security.

Now, as a PhD student, I'm pushing the boundaries of LLM privacy research—developing novel attack methodologies and exploring how we can ensure responsible deployment of generative AI systems. Python remains my trusty sidekick throughout this journey!
